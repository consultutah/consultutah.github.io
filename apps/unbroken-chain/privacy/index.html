Unbroken Chain

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Unbroken Chain is designed as a private, local-first iPhone habit tracker for creating goals, choosing tracked weekdays, marking completed days, viewing streaks, and using Home Screen widgets.

Data you enter in the app

You may enter goal names, selected weekdays, completion dates, reminder settings, and subscription-related app state. This information is stored on your device. The app may also store a limited dashboard snapshot in its Apple App Group container so the Unbroken Chain widget can display your current progress.

Data sent to Consult Utah

Consult Utah does not operate a custom backend for Unbroken Chain habit data. Your goals, completion history, streaks, and reminder settings are not sent to Consult Utah servers by the app.

Purchases and subscriptions

Unbroken Chain may offer optional Premium features through Apple in-app purchases. Purchase and subscription transactions are processed by Apple using StoreKit so the app can unlock Premium features and restore purchases. Consult Utah does not receive your full payment card details.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Unbroken Chain may schedule local notifications on your device for the goals and weekdays you choose. Notification settings can be managed in iOS Settings.

Tracking and advertising

Unbroken Chain does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Support requests

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, screenshots, device details, or description of the issue.
