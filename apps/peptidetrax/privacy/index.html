Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

PeptideTrax is designed as a private, local-first iPhone app for tracking peptide schedules, dose logs, inventory, reminders, and reports.

Medical disclaimer

PeptideTrax is an organizational tracking tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, dosage recommendations, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.

Data you enter in the app

You may enter peptide names, schedules, dose logs, inventory details, notes, vial photos, reminder preferences, and report data. This information is stored on your device. If you enable Apple iCloud features, Apple may sync app data through your iCloud account according to Apple’s terms and privacy practices.

Data sent to Consult Utah

Consult Utah does not operate a custom backend for PeptideTrax personal tracking data. Your peptide logs, inventory, notes, and reports are not sent to Consult Utah servers by the app.

Purchases and subscriptions

PeptideTrax may offer optional premium features through Apple in-app purchases. Purchase and subscription status may be processed by Apple and RevenueCat so the app can unlock premium features, restore purchases, and manage entitlements. Consult Utah does not receive your full payment card details.

Photos and OCR

If you choose to add vial photos or use label autofill/OCR features, those features are intended to process information on device where supported. Do not add photos or notes you do not want stored on your device or synced through your Apple account.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, PeptideTrax may schedule local notifications on your device. Notification settings can be managed in iOS Settings.

Support requests

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, screenshots, device details, or description of the issue.

Contact
